Our Response To The Coronavirus Pandemic

During the pandemic, DrCare aims to reduce the pressure on patients, pharmacists, and doctors through facilitating and sharing prescriptions online.
With our system, patients will no longer need to meet their GP in person, to get a repeat prescription.
Our online prescriptions will enable patients to get their prescription from the pharmacist without presenting a paper script.
All of these is aligned with the mandate from HSE to help reduce the spread of coronavirus

What Our System Offers

  Safe, Fast and Convenient
  No more missed information
  No more illegible handwritten prescriptions
  Conveniently pay via PayPal
  Pickup medications from pharmacy of your choice
